About Us

Mission

Our work empowers and uplifts our community to fight for racial and economic justice and put a stop to the criminalization of our people. We fight for the self-determination and sovereignty of SWANA folks under attack in Palestine, Yemen, Iraq, Syria, Lebanon, and across the entire SWANA region with the same breath that we fight for our rights here in the United States.

We see our struggles here as a domestic manifestation of global militarism and capitalism, directly connected to the struggles of our people in our homelands. What happens in our homelands has a direct impact on what happens to our communities here in the United States.

We are also committed to and actively developing interventions to heal the generational trauma grown from patriarchy and white supremacy that prevent our community from taking effective action towards the transformation and liberation of our people.
